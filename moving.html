Moving Tips

We are here to help

Reuse

Collecting boxes from friends and local businesses is a cost-saving solution, and purchasing used boxes is another option to consider when moving on a budget.

Plan Ahead

Before you start your move, have a checklist to help you decide what items you need to pack and what items you may want to consider donating or leaving behind. Moving during the weekend or on a small scale can often result in less stress and lower costs than moving during the busy weekday rush hours.
